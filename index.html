About us

Nestled high in the majestic Alps, Marmot Studios is the brainchild of two adventurous marmots with a passion for mobile apps development. When we're not busy nibbling on alpine flowers or skiing on giraffes, we're creating awesome apps.

Oh, and did we mention? We despise ads as much as a marmot detests a rainy day! That's why our apps are proudly ad-free, because who needs ads when you're busy having fun?

Our Projects

Chess clock

Every second matters! This app adds excitement to your games and keeps you on the edge of your seat!

  • Multiple time controls built-in - start playing with a single click!
  • Customize time controls with increments and asymmetric options
  • Dark mode - easier on the eyes and your phone battery
  • Customizable colors for a personalized experience

Board game timer

Speed up your game nights! This app keeps everyone on their toes by preventing overthinking, adding excitement and keeping the game moving.

  • Offers multiple time controls: counting up, time-per-move, and time-per-game
  • Save and quickly re-add friends for future games
  • Choose favorite color for every player with our intuitive color picker
  • Save games and resume them later, in any order
  • Access detailed statistics anytime: average turn time per player, slowest turn, and more
  • Dark mode - gentler on your eyes and phone battery
